I am an individual who loves creating, coding, and Cooking! My previous career was that of a Head Chef at a Café and Lounge for 7 years. I took this as an opportunity to explore new and old passions and I soon found myself gravitating to an old hobby that I had many years ago; creating web pages! After enjoying a boot camp paired with LinkedIn Learning, I had decided that I wanted to jump back into the game.
